Problem:

    A right triangle ABC, with a midpoint on the hypotenuse, AC, called M. Given the lengths of AB and BC, find the angle MBC in degrees. 



Input Format:

    The first line is the length of side AB and second line contains the length of side BC.



Constraints:

    AB and BC are natural numbers
    0 <= AB <= 100
    0 <= BC <= 100
